Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the interconnected network of physical devices that communicate and exchange data over the internet. This technology enables everyday objects, from home appliances to industrial machines, to collect and share data, leading to smarter operations and improved efficiency.

IoT is transforming industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and transportation, facilitating real-time monitoring and automation. With the growing adoption of IoT devices, we are moving towards a more connected world where data drives decision-making and innovation.

Moreover, IoT applications are enhancing user experiences, from smart homes that learn your preferences to connected vehicles that improve safety and efficiency on the road. As technology evolves, the potential of IoT continues to expand, promising innovative solutions to complex challenges.
